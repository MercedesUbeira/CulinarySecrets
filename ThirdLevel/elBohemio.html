Bellagamba
About
Nestled in the heart of Buenos Aires, Bellagamba is a captivating bodegón that transcends time. Adorned with vintage photographs and antique elements, this hidden gem takes diners on an enchanting journey through the city's rich history while tantalizing their taste buds with exquisite regional cuisine. The warm ambiance and rustic charm of Bellagamba create an inviting atmosphere, making it a cherished spot for both locals and visitors seeking an authentic gastronomic experience.
Price Range
The menu caters to diverse tastes, ensuring a delightful dining experience for every budget. Prices typically range from 15USD to 30USD, offering a variety of options for discerning diners.
